New York City (NYC) is the most populous city in the United States, located at the southern tip of New York State. Known as “The Big Apple” and “The City That Never Sleeps,” it is a global hub for finance, culture, entertainment, and diplomacy. The city is made up of five boroughs—Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island—and is home to iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, Central Park, and the Empire State Building. With over 800 languages spoken, NYC is the most linguistically diverse city in the world
